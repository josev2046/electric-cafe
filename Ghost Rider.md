# Suicide - "Ghost Rider" 

Operational steps to perform "Ghost Rider" using a serial signal path. The **Arturia MiniBrute** serves as the master clock and keyboard, the **Behringer TD-3-MO** provides the hypnotic bass, and the **Korg MS2000BR** handles the lead textures.

---

## The Performance Blueprint
* **Tempo:** 124 BPM
* **Key:** C Major
* **The Vibe:** Hypnotic, repetitive, and increasingly distorted.

---

## Step 1: Set the "Nerves" (The Sync)
* **MiniBrute:** Turn the unit on. This acts as your conductor/master clock.
* **TD-3-MO:** Set the clock source to MIDI (Hold `BACK` + `WRITE/NEXT` and press `Step 2`).
* **Korg MS2000BR:** Ensure the MIDI Global channel is set to **Channel 1**.

---

## Step 2: Program the "Throb" (The TD-3-MO)
*You require a consistent 16-step "pulse".*

1.  **Clear Pattern:** Press `FUNCTION` + `PATTERN CLEAR`.
2.  **Pitch Mode:** Press `PITCH MODE`.
3.  **Enter Notes:** Press the **C key** 16 times (16 taps).
4.  **Time Mode:** Press `TIME MODE`.
5.  **Enter Rhythm:** Press the **Note Button** (located next to the Tie) 16 times.
6.  **Finalise:** Press `RUN`. You should hear a steady, rhythmic "donk-donk-donk-donk."

---

## Step 3: Sculpt the "Grime" (The MiniBrute)
*The TD-3 signal is now flowing through the MiniBrute’s Steiner-Parker filter.*

1.  **The Switch:** Locate the **Gate Source** switch on the rear panel. Set it to **HOLD**. (The TD-3 audio should now pass through without requiring a key-press).
2.  **The Mixer:** On the front panel, turn the **Audio In** fader to 100%. Set all other internal oscillators (Saw, Square, etc.) to 0%.
3.  **The Filter:**
    * **Cutoff:** 20% (Low).
    * **Resonance:** 20%.
    * **Brute Factor:** 50%.
4.  **The Result:** A heavy, muffled, and distorted analog pulse.

---

## Step 4: Prepare the "Void" (The Korg)
1.  **Select Patch:** Choose a thin, "cheesy" transistor organ or a high-pitched "Sine" lead.
2.  **Effects:** Increase the **Delay** level. Aim for a "slapback" echo with approximately 2–3 repeats.

---

## Step 5: The Performance Routine

### 0:00 – The Intro
* Press **RUN** on the TD-3-MO.
* The bass should play in isolation, sounding dark and muffled.
* **Action:** Slowly—over the course of 30 seconds—turn the **MiniBrute Cutoff** knob from 20% to 60%. Listen for the "Ghost Rider" grit to emerge as the filter opens.

### 0:30 – The Lead Hook
* Using the **MiniBrute Keyboard**, play these two notes in a high octave (triggering the Korg):
    * **Note 1:** **G** (Hold for 2 beats)
    * **Note 2:** **E** (Hold for 2 beats)
* Repeat this cycle. This is the primary eerie melody of the track.

### 1:00 – The Tension
* Continue playing the **G** and **E** notes with your right hand.
* Use your left hand to manipulate the **MiniBrute Resonance**.
* Sweep the Resonance up to 80% to induce "screaming" feedback, then return it to 20%.

### 1:30 – The Alan Vega "Shout"
* If a microphone is connected to the Korg, perform the "breathless" vocal style.
* If no microphone is present, focus on the **Brute Factor** knob. Crank it to 100% for 4 bars to create an "audio explosion," then return to 50%.

### The Finish
* Abruptly turn the **MiniBrute Cutoff** to 0% to create instant silence.
* Stop the **TD-3-MO** sequencer.
